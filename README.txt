-Description-

TLRanks is a chrome extension which displays ladder information for users of the teamliquid.net forum. This is accomplished by using sc2ranks.com to make a best guess at matching forum usernames with a corresponding battle.net username and parsing their profile for their league, race, and other relevant information.


-Instructions-

To load the unpacked extension:
1. Open Chrome settings and click the "Extensions" tab.
2. Check the "Developer mode" checkbox in the top right.
3. Click the "Load unpacked extension..." button and select the folder containing the plugin.

After installing, forum users matching sc2ranks profile will have a link to their profile and league icon displayed next to their name.
